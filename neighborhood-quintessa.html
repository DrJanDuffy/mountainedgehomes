About Quintessa

Quintessa stands as one of Mountain's Edge's premier communities, offering architectural distinction and luxurious living. This carefully planned neighborhood features elegant homes with premium finishes and impressive design elements.

Constructed between 2006 and 2013, Quintessa homes showcase exceptional craftsmanship with designer kitchens, high ceilings, and expansive living spaces. The community's premium location provides stunning mountain views and convenient access to amenities.

Neighborhood Highlights

  • Home Types: Luxury single-family homes
  • Square Footage: 2,800 - 4,600 sq ft
  • Year Built: 2006-2013
  • Price Range: $700,000 - $1,000,000
  • Security: Gated entrance, security patrols
  • Amenities: Community center, park, walking trails
