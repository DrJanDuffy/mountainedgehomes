About Collina

Collina is one of Mountain's Edge's most sought-after communities, known for its stunning homes and picturesque setting. With beautiful mountain views and meticulously maintained landscapes, Collina offers an elevated living experience.

Built between 2007 and 2014, homes in Collina feature contemporary designs with premium finishes, spacious layouts, and energy-efficient features. The community's proximity to parks, trails, and local amenities makes it ideal for families and active individuals.

Neighborhood Highlights

  • Home Types: Single-family homes
  • Square Footage: 2,400 - 4,200 sq ft
  • Year Built: 2007-2014
  • Price Range: $600,000 - $850,000
  • Security: HOA security patrols
  • Amenities: Community park, playground, walking trails
