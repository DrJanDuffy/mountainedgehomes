Mesa/Valla

Family-Friendly Community in Mountain's Edge

About Mesa/Valla

Mesa/Valla represents two connected communities within Mountain's Edge that offer excellent value and family-friendly living. These neighborhoods feature attractive homes with desert-inspired architecture and thoughtful community planning.

Built primarily between 2008 and 2015, Mesa/Valla homes include both single and two-story designs with modern amenities, open floor plans, and energy-efficient features. The community's parks, schools, and recreational areas make it perfect for families.

Neighborhood Highlights

  • Home Types: Single-family homes
  • Square Footage: 1,800 - 3,200 sq ft
  • Year Built: 2008-2015
  • Price Range: $450,000 - $650,000
  • Security: HOA security patrols
  • Amenities: Community parks, playgrounds, walking trails
