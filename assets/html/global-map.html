About Mountain Edge

Mountain Edge is a master-planned community located in southwest Las Vegas, Nevada. Known for its scenic mountain views, beautiful homes, and numerous parks and trails, Mountain Edge offers a high quality of life and easy access to major amenities.

The community features multiple neighborhoods with various home styles and price points, making it accessible to a wide range of homebuyers.
