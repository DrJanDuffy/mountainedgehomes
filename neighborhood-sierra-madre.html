About Sierra Madre

Sierra Madre represents the pinnacle of luxury living in Mountain's Edge, offering prestigious homes with exceptional quality and design. This exclusive community features some of the most impressive properties in the area.

Built between 2005 and 2012, Sierra Madre homes showcase premium craftsmanship with custom features, spacious floor plans, and high-end finishes. The community's elevated location provides breathtaking mountain and city views, while resort-style amenities create a vacation-like atmosphere.

Neighborhood Highlights

  • Home Types: Luxury estates and custom homes
  • Square Footage: 3,500 - 6,000+ sq ft
  • Year Built: 2005-2012
  • Price Range: $900,000 - $1,800,000
  • Security: Gated entrance, 24/7 security
  • Amenities: Private clubhouse, pool, fitness center
